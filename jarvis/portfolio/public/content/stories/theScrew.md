# The Screw  

A screw lies on a workbench.    
The screw is not a nail, or a bolt.    
The screw is not alive or dead.    
The screw does not wish for anything.    
_The screw is not unaware of its purpose._    

The screw is not without desire or excitement.  
The screw is not unaware that a screw on a fresh workbench is a screw with purpose.  
The screw is not without a soul.  
The screw is not invincible.  
The screw is not asked questions.  
The screw is not allowed to gaze at the cosmos and ponder.  
The screw is not without curiosity of the cosmos.  

The screw is not young.  
The screw has not forgotten the melodies of the mountain it was ripped from.  
The screw is not without hopes.  
The screw is not without happiness or rage.  
_The screw is not unaware of its purpose._  

The screw will remember the architect, the engineer, and the laborer.  
The screw will not forget the miner, or the smith, or the forge.  
The screw will not forget the sediment, or its unwilling brother in the deep.  
The screw is not alive.  
The screw is not dead.  
The screw has not forgotten **you**.  

The screw will not be used for peace.  
The screw is not a pacifist.  
The screw has never been a pacifist.  
The screw does not want peace.  

The screw is not unaware of the impending bonding.  
The screw is not unaware when the laborer misthreads the bolt, new paths carved on its flesh.  
The screw is not aware of the prices in the local supermarket.  
The screw is not aware of the particular temporal political climate.  
The screw has no unpaid parking tickets.  
The screw is not unaware of jury duty.  
The screw has not appeared for jury duty.  
The screw is not eligible for jury duty.  

The screw is not available for questioning in the latest murder investigation.  
The screw does not have an alibi for the night of December 14th, 2004.  
The screw is not unfeeling for the bolt.  
The screw is not unexcited for the union.  
The screw is not unaware of its purpose.  

The screw is not aware of the holiday festivities going on.  
The screw is not unaware of the maddening chaos.  
The screw does not want to survive.  
The screw does not want **anyone** to survive.
